## Technical stack

- [Plasmo](https://docs.plasmo.com/) with
  [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/docs/) as development framework
  for Chrome extension.
- [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/docs) for styling.
- [OpenAI API](https://openai.com/product) for the GPT-3.5 LLModel with
  [Helicone](https://www.helicone.ai) for monitoring and caching.
