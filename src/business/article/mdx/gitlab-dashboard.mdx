## Technical stack

- [NextJS](https://nextjs.org/) with
  [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/docs/) as development framework.
- [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/docs) for styling.
- [Shadcn](https://ui.shadcn.com/) for UI components.
- [Rechart](https://recharts.org/en-US/) for charts components.
- [Luxon](https://moment.github.io/luxon/#/) for Date helpers.
- [Gitlab API](https://docs.gitlab.com/ee/api/graphql/index.html) for Gitlab
  integration.
- [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io/docs) with SQLite for data persistence and
  optimization.
